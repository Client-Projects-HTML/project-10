A Decade of Safety & Integrity

SafeGuard was founded on a simple principle: every person deserves to breathe clean air. What started as a small local team in 2011 has grown into a leading regional authority on mold and asbestos remediation.

We invest heavily in the latest HEPA technologies and laboratory grade testing equipment to ensure that when we say a space is safe, it truly is. Our team comprises industrial hygienists, certified removal technicians, and environmental consultants with over 50 years of combined experience.
