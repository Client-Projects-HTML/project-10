Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know about our service plans.

Can I get a discount for multiple tests?

Yes, we offer special multi-unit pricing for real-estate professionals and property managers. Our volume discounts start for projects requiring more than 5 individual tests or samples.

What is included in the 'Base Price'?

Base pricing covers the mobilization of the crew, heavy-duty equipment setup, and disposal fees for areas up to 200 sq. ft. For larger properties, we provide a fixed per-square-foot rate.

How long does a Lab Report take?

Our standard turnaround is 24-48 hours. Expedited reports (8-hour turnaround) are available for an additional $75 fee per sample, subject to lab availability.
